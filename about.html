Jay (AKA BLACKDISC) is a multidisciplinary creative that explores the intersection between music production/history, video games & culture.

He's a multi-instrumentalist, writer, video essayist dj and all around silly guy. He also thinks that writing about himself in the third person is weird but kind of fun.

His current goal is to build the entertainment/music scene infrastucture in his community through event planning and community outreach.
